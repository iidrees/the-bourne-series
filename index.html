Fun Facts about the Bourne Movie Franchise

  • There are 13 Books in the franchise and 5 movies

  • The Bourne Legacy book was totally different from the Movie

  • The Bourne Identity movie almost changed its plot because of the 9/11 disaster

  • The screenwriters of the Bourne Identity movie only used a tiny fraction of the plot from the book missing a chance to use an original story.

  • Matt Demon was not the first choice for the Jason Bourne role.

  • In the Bourne Legacy book, Jason bourne is not the only graduate of the Treadstone program.

  • The latest Bourne movie series are not the first adaptation of the Jason Bourne franchise
